MEMORANDUM OF POINTS AND AUTHORITIES

In Support of Petition for Dismissal

I. STATEMENT OF FACTS

Petitioner was convicted of violating Health & Safety Code ¬ß 11377(a) on March 15, 2021. He was sentenced to 3 years formal probation, which he successfully completed on March 15, 2024, without any violations.

II. LEGAL AUTHORITY

A. Statutory Authority

Penal Code ¬ß 1203.4(a)(1) provides:

"In any case in which a defendant has fulfilled the conditions of probation for the entire period of probation... the defendant shall, at any time after the termination of the period of probation... be permitted by the court to withdraw his or her plea of guilty... and... the court shall thereupon dismiss the accusations or information against the defendant..."

B. Eligibility Requirements Met

Petitioner meets all statutory requirements:

  1. Successful completion of probation (PC ¬ß 1203.4(a)(1))
  2. Not currently charged with any offense (PC ¬ß 1203.4(a)(1))
  3. Not serving a sentence for any offense (PC ¬ß 1203.4(a)(1))
  4. Not on probation for any offense (PC ¬ß 1203.4(a)(1))

III. INTERESTS OF JUSTICE STANDARD

Courts have broad discretion in determining whether dismissal is in the interests of justice. People v. McLernon (2009) 174 Cal.App.4th 569, 576. Factors include:

  • ‚Ä¢ Nature and severity of the offense
  • ‚Ä¢ Defendant's criminal history
  • ‚Ä¢ Defendant's rehabilitation efforts
  • ‚Ä¢ Impact on defendant's livelihood

IV. ARGUMENT

The interests of justice strongly favor granting this petition:

  1. Complete Rehabilitation: Petitioner has maintained sobriety for 3+ years, completed treatment, and transformed his life.
  2. Productive Member of Society: Steady employment, pursuing education, supporting family.
  3. No Public Safety Risk: No arrests or violations since conviction.
  4. Compelling Need: Conviction limits employment and housing opportunities.

V. CONCLUSION

For the foregoing reasons, Petitioner respectfully requests the Court grant this petition and dismiss the conviction pursuant to Penal Code ¬ß 1203.4.
